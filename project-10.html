For Play

Category : iOS
Client : Apple App Store
Completion : 1.5 Released July 2021

This is a service that allows for people to meet up for pickup sports, hikes, yoga, etc. With the back-end service discontining service (Parse.com) the project has been migrated to Google using Firebase's FireStorm & Auth. The Ui was redone wtih SwiftUI as well! The sourcecode lives at: https://github.com/keithmacinnis/for-play and you can download the app at: https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/for-play/id1014975395
