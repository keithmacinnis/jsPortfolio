Purchaces with Bitcoin & Shakepay

Posted by Keith MacInnis July 23, 2021 Crypto

Purchasing goods online with bitcoin can be uneasy your first time! I have here a short gudie.

The store I used had a btc checkout, when you clicked it you had one hour to pay and were presented with a qr code.

The QR code ensures you do not mistype the address.

But how do you pay? If you have money in your digital wallet its easy. But if its your first time, there are two extra steps. 1) Download a wallet. 2) Load your wallet with cash. If you're Canadian, checkout ShakePay here. To fund it, you send an interac e-mail which processes in a minute. You can then convert from Cdn -> Bitcon/Eth and then you can pay any retalier taking crypto.
